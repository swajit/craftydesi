# Mandir Design 3

Ideal for smaller homes or apartments, this compact mandir provides a dedicated sacred space without overwhelming the room. Its minimalist design highlights the natural beauty of the wood while providing ample space for daily rituals.

## Features
- **Compact Footprint:** Fits perfectly in bedrooms or multi-purpose spaces.
- **Elegant Proportions:** Balanced design that feels light and airy.
- **Natural Birch Grain:** Showcases the authentic beauty of the material.
- **Built-in Shelving:** Perfect for small lamps and puja items.

## The Process
Designed with a focus on simplicity and ease of assembly. We utilized CNC-style precision manually to ensure every joint is perfect.
