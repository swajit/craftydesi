# Pani Poori Stall

Bringing the joy of Indian street food home. This custom-built stall is designed for hosting and serving Pani Poori in style, featuring dedicated spaces for all the essential components.

## Features
- **Themed Design:** Captures the essence of a traditional street food cart.
- **Integrated Serving Surfaces:** Perfect heights for serving and preparation.
- **Sanitary Surfaces:** Easy-to-clean finishes for food safety.
- **Mobile Foundation:** Can be moved easily for indoor or outdoor parties.

## The Process
We wanted this to feel authentic yet high-end. The proportions were taken from traditional carts but refined with modern joinery and materials.
