# Built-in Mandir 2

A contemporary take on traditional sacred spaces, this second iteration of our built-in mandirs focuses on light and accessibility. The design emphasizes clean horizontal lines and a modern aesthetic while respecting traditional requirements.

## Features
- **Modern Proportions:** Slim profiles and spacious shelves.
- **Versatile Storage:** Includes drawers for puja essentials.
- **Premium Finish:** Smooth, natural texture that highlights the wood grain.
- **Seamless Integration:** Designed to look like a natural extension of the room.

## The Process
Built using advanced woodworking techniques to ensure structural stability within a compact footprint. Each unit is custom-measured for its specific space.
