# Heritage Teak Spice Box

This traditional teak spice box is a tribute to heritage craftsmanship, reimagined for the modern kitchen. Featuring intricate hand-carved joinery, it's as much a piece of art as it is a functional kitchen accessory.

## Details
- **Premium Teak Wood:** Known for its durability and stunning golden-brown hue.
- **Intricate Joinery:** Crafted using traditional methods for a unique look and feel.
- **Food-Safe Finish:** Treated with high-quality mineral oils to ensure safety.
- **Compact & Functional:** Designed to hold a variety of spices in an organized, beautiful way.

## Why This Piece?
The Heritage Teak Spice Box is about bringing a touch of history into your daily cooking routine. It's a celebration of the materials and the skills passed down through generations.
