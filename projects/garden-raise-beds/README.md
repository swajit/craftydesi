# Garden Raised Beds

Elevate your gardening game with these custom-built raised beds. Designed for optimal drainage and ergonomic access, these beds make growing your own vegetables and flowers a joy.

## Features
- **Ergonomic Height:** Reduces back strain during planting and weeding.
- **Superior Drainage:** Designed to keep soil healthy and productive.
- **Modular Design:** Can be arranged in various configurations to fit your garden.
- **Cedar-Lined Walls:** Naturally resistant to rot and pests.

## The Process
We focused on longevity, using stainless steel fasteners and weather-treated foundations. From the first seed to the mid-season harvest, these beds provide a perfect environment for growth.
