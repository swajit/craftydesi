# Traditional Stained Mandir (Design 5)

A tall, majestic mandir finished in a deep walnut stain that evokes a sense of timeless tradition. This piece is designed to be the centerpiece of a dedicated prayer room, with ample storage and a grand presence.

## Features
- **Deep Stain Finish:** Rich, dark tones that highlight the wood grain.
- **Tall Profile:** Provides a grand backdrop for worship.
- **Integrated Storage:** Lower cabinets for prayer books and accessories.
- **Custom Moulding:** Elegant trim details for a premium look.

## The Process
The transformation from raw birch to this rich walnut finish involved multiple stages of pre-stain treatment and hand-rubbed finishing to achieve the perfect depth of color.
