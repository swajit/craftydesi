# Sun-Kissed Cedar Swing

Crafted from western red cedar, this porch swing combines ergonomic design with the natural durability of cedar. Perfect for lazy afternoons on the porch.

## Features
- **Ergonomic Curves:** Designed for maximum comfort without the need for cushions.
- **Weather-Resistant Cedar:** Naturally resistant to rot and insects.
- **Stainless Steel Hardware:** For long-lasting outdoor use.
- **Hand-Sanded Finish:** A silky smooth surface that feels great to the touch.

## Inspiration
I wanted to create a piece that makes you want to sit down and stay a while. The natural scent of cedar is a bonus that brings a touch of the forest to your home.
