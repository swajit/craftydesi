# Obsidian Oak Serving Tray

This serving tray is finished using the traditional Japanese *shou sugi ban* technique, charring the surface to create a deep, textured black finish that highlights the oak's grain.

## Details
- **Shou Sugi Ban Finish:** Hand-charred, brushed, and sealed with food-safe oils.
- **Premium Oak:** Provides a sturdy and durable base for serving.
- **Hand-Forged Handles:** Adding a touch of industrial elegance.
- **Unique Texture:** The charring process makes every tray one-of-a-kind.

## The Aesthetic
The Obsidian Tray is for those who appreciate the beauty of contrasting textures. It's a bold statement piece for any dining table or bar cart.
