# Minimalist Ash Nightstand

A floating nightstand that maximizes space while maintaining a clean, modern aesthetic. Crafted from light ash wood, it features a hidden drawer for a clutter-free bedside.

## Specifications
- **Floating Design:** Attaches securely to the wall for a sleek, modern look.
- **Ash Wood:** Known for its strength and beautiful light color.
- **Hidden Drawer:** Keeps your essentials close but out of sight.
- **Smooth Glide:** High-quality drawer slides for effortless use.

## Design Philosophy
Less is truly more. This nightstand is designed to disappear into the room, leaving only the warmth and texture of the wood on display.
